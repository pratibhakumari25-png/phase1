The Small Seed

Author: Pratibha Kumari | Published: Oct 22, 2025

Story

A farmer planted a tiny seed. Other plants laughed, but the seed kept growing quietly underground. Over time, it sprouted and became a huge banyan tree, providing shade to the whole village.

“Grow slowly, but grow deeply.”

Lesson: Patience and persistence lead to success.
