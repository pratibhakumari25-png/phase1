Masala tea**

    Ingredients:-

  1. 1 cup water
  2. 1 cup milk
  3. 1–2 teaspoons tea leaves
  4. 1 small piece ginger (crushed)
  5. 2–3 cardamoms (crushed)
  6. 1 small piece cinnamon (optional)
  7. 2 cloves (optional)
  8. Sugar to taste

Instructions:-

  • Pour 1 cup of water into a pan and heat it
    • v
  • Add 1 small piece of crushed ginger and 2–3 crushed cardamoms
  • For extra flavor, you can also add 1 small piece of cinnamon and 2 cloves
  • Add 1–2 teaspoons of tea leaves and let the water boil
  • When the water turns dark, add 1 cup of milk
  • Add sugar to taste
  • Let it boil for 2–3 minutes so that the tea and spices mix well
  • Turn off the heat and strain the tea using a sieve
  • Serve the hot masala tea
